RAMADAN 1444 AH

First day of Ramadan is Thursday, March 23, 2023. The first night of Tarawih will be on Wednesday, March 22, 2023. Salaatul Isha timing is below. The doors will open 10 minutes before Isha iqaama daily, inshaAllah. There will be a after witr Dars daily. Please do not bring any food items from home, we do not have the facilities. Dates and water will be provided by masjid, iA. May Allah grant us all a blessed Ramadan.

Iqama Timings


Alhamdulillah, by the grace and mercy of Allah SWT, Isha salaat and Taraweeh started at Masjid Ar-Raheem on Friday, April 1, 2022 / Ramadan 1, 1443. With the main construction complete, we now offer 5 times daily salaat.

Once the renovation work is completed for the Masjid (325 Rodeo Ave), planning and development of a school/multipurpose building will commence, inshaAllah, for the adjacent property (338 Parker Ave) purchased by Masjid Ar-Raheem.

May Allah reward everyone who has contributed towards this noble cause. Your continued duas and support are needed.
